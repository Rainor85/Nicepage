Jin & Jan

​Jin & Jan Jin & Jan is a skill game in which you control two characters moving at the same time but in opposite directions. Players must reach the center of the level while dodging obstacles and enemies in order to unite their souls.Recognition:  Rank 7 in the "mechanic" and "general" categories in GSDJIII.

Genre: Skill and Puzzle
Engine: Unreal Engine 4
Team Size: 5
Paltform: PC
Realese :  Currently in development
